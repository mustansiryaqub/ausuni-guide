Before You Request Deletion

You can request deletion of your AusUni Guide account and personal data at any time. Please read the following before submitting:

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, your checklist progress, preferences, and account will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
💡 If you have an active premium subscription, please cancel it first through Google Play before requesting data deletion. Data deletion does not automatically cancel your subscription or issue a refund.

What Happens Next

  • Request received — We log your deletion request and send you a confirmation email within 24 hours.

  • Identity verified — We may ask you to verify your identity to protect against unauthorised deletion requests.

  • Data deleted — All your personal data is permanently removed from our systems (Supabase database) within 30 days.

  • Confirmation sent — We send you a final confirmation email once deletion is complete.

  • Subscription cancelled — Remember to also cancel your Google Play subscription separately if applicable.

✅ We are committed to processing all deletion requests promptly and in full compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and GDPR where applicable.
❓

Frequently Asked Questions

Will deletion cancel my premium subscription?
No. You must cancel your subscription separately through Google Play Settings → Subscriptions. Data deletion only removes your personal data from our servers.

Can I recover my data after deletion?
No. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting or noting any information you wish to keep before submitting a request.

How long does it take?
We process all deletion requests within 30 days. In most cases it is completed within 7 days. You will receive an email confirmation when complete.

What about ARIA conversation history?
ARIA conversations are not stored on our servers beyond your active session. There is no persistent conversation history to delete.

Can I create a new account after deleting?
Yes. You may create a new AusUni Guide account at any time after your deletion is processed.
