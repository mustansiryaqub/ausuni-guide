Overview

AusUni Guide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the AusUni Guide mobile application ("App").

AusUni Guide is designed to help international students and their families discover Australian universities, find suitable courses, explore scholarships, understand accommodation options, and access student job resources.

✅ We are committed to collecting only the minimum information necessary to provide you with a great experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
📊

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Messages and queries you send to ARIA (our AI assistant)
  • Checklist progress and preferences you save within the app
  • City and accommodation preferences in the Cost of Living Calculator
  • Account information if you create an account (email address)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • General usage analytics (screens visited, features used)
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability
  • Purchase and subscription status (via RevenueCat)
⚠️ We do not collect your name, phone number, home address, passport details, bank account information, or any sensitive identity documents.
🤖

AI-Powered Features (ARIA)

AusUni Guide includes ARIA, an AI assistant powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you interact with ARIA:

  • Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses
  • Conversations are processed in real time and are not permanently stored by us beyond your active session
  • Anthropic may process your messages in accordance with their own Privacy Policy
  • Do not share sensitive personal information (passport numbers, bank details, passwords) in ARIA conversations

You can review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

💡 ARIA is designed to help you find educational opportunities. All university, scholarship, and job information is curated and updated regularly, but we recommend verifying details directly with institutions.
💳

Premium Subscriptions

AusUni Guide offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Google Play Store and managed by RevenueCat. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment is processed entirely by Google Play — we never receive or store your payment card details
  • RevenueCat receives your anonymised purchase receipt to manage subscription entitlements
  • Your subscription status is stored to unlock premium features
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play account settings

Review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

🔍

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the AusUni Guide app and its features
  • Power the ARIA AI assistant responses
  • Save your checklist progress and personalised preferences
  • Manage your premium subscription status
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

🤝

Information Sharing

We share limited information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the App:

Anthropic AI conversation processing (ARIA)
RevenueCat Subscription management
Google Play Payment processing
Supabase Secure cloud data storage (checklist & preferences)

Each third party is bound by their own privacy policy and data protection standards. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other parties.

🔒

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider with enterprise-grade security. We implement the following safeguards:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • User data is protected by Row Level Security — you can only access your own data
  • We retain your data only for as long as your account remains active
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us
🛡️ We store data on servers located in Australia and the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer and processing.
👧

Children's Privacy

AusUni Guide is designed for students aged 16 and above, as well as their parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will promptly delete such information.

Users between 13–16 years should use the app under parental supervision. We encourage parents and guardians to explore the app together with their children when researching Australian study options.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days.
